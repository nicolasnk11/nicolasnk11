### 👨‍💻 About Me & My Journey

I'm from Fortaleza (CE), Brazil, and my passion is transforming ideas into digital reality, from code to visual identity. I navigate smoothly between design and solid software development.

- 🎓 **Educational Foundation:** Graduated as an **IT Technician** from **IFCE** (Federal Institute of Ceará).
- 🏛️ **Undergrad:** Currently studying **Systems and Digital Media (SMD)** at **UFC**.
- 💼 **Experience:** Entrepreneur in the men's grooming sector (Castro Barber) with previous experience in administrative management.
- 🎯 **Current Focus:** Developing full-stack web applications (like SAMI and LVCVETSUS) and studying modern Front and Back-end stacks.
- ⚡ **Beyond the code:** In my free time, you can find me playing futevôlei on the sand or enjoying some PS4 gameplay.
